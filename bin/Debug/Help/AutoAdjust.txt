LABEL1=
Auto Adjust Selection can make BMTune auto adjust
the fuel maps automatically. If will read the AFR,
if the conditions are met, the AFR are saved in the
AFR Reading display, and BMTune caculated the fuel
difference to add or remove at the specific location.

Fuel Difference percentage can be seen in the tables
menu, exactly in the the views tab named 'Fuel Difference'.

Condition for AFR Reading can be set in the Settings,
in the 'Overlay(Afr)' tab.
!LABEL1
