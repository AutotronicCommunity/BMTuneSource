LABEL1=
The 2D/3D Graph show visually your tables. You can
quickly see how smooth your maps is or see if the
maps kinda make non sense.

You can select region by doing a single clic or by
selecting a whole region on the graph when dragging.

You can move from point to point quickly with your
shortcuts keys to make your editing faster and easier.

The 2D/3D Graph generally refer to your Fuel and Ignitions
tables of your baserom.
!LABEL1
