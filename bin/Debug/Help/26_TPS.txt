You can set a custom TPS voltage reading if you want or simply
syncronize your current TPS sensor perfectly with the baserom.

If you want to sync your TPS sensor, you will need to have the ecu
connected to the software (datalogging).

You must have the engine stopped but the Ignition Key turned ON.

Follow the specified step to set your TPS voltage.
1. set your TPS at 0% (no throttle), then clic next
2. set your TPS at 100% (full throttle), then clic next again.

Your TPS voltage will be set in the baserom.
!LABEL1
