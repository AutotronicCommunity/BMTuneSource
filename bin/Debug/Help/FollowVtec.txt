LABEL1=
Follow Vtec will follow and switch tables automatically
using the datalog. This mean if you are doing a live tuning
sesion, and your ecu pass the conditions to switch maps, by
exemple you hit the Vtec, then follow vtec will switch the
shown table in BMTune automaticaly between the low and high cam.

It will follow the datalog cursor if it switch between Low
and High cam.
!LABEL1
