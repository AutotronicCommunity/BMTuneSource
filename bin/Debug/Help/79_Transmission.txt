LABEL1=
This menu will let you choose your current transmission to put
in the baserom. This can be used to know which gear the car are
running on.

You can put a custom transmisson ratio if your transmission are
not listed and customized. The value are raw values but they tend
to be pretty similar that 'km/h' values.

Gear Learn Tool can help you to set a custom transmission that you
don't know what raw value to use.
